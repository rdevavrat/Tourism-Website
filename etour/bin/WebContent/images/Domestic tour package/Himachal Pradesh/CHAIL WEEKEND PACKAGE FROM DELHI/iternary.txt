DELHI TO CHAIL (346KMS)
The trip starts from Delhi and goes up to Chail where upon arrival you check in at hotel. Night stay at hotel.

After breakfast leave for an excursion of chail which covers the most renowned destinations like Chail palace, Chail sanctuary and many more. Come back to hotel for overnight stay.

Morning will leave for Delhi for your next destination.
