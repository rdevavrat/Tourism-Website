ITINERARY
Itinerary

ARRIVE DELHI
Upon arrival in Delhi, meet our representatives who will transfer you to a hotel. Overnight stay at the Delhi hotel.


DELHI
In the morning, go for a sightseeing tour of Delhi. Visit Raj Ghat (the cremation sites of Mahatma Gandhi), Shanti Vana (the cremation sites of Jawaharlal Nehru) and Jama Masjid. Drive past Red Fort and Chandni Chowk. In the afternoon, visit Qutub Minar (built by Qutub-ud-Din Aibek in 1199), Humayun's Tomb, India Gate (War Memorial Arch) and Lakshminarayan Temple (a modern Hindu Temple). Also drive past President's House, Parliament House, Government Secretariat Buildings and Connaught Place shopping center. Stay overnight at the hotel.


DELHI - AGRA (205 KILOMETERS; 4 HOURS)
In the morning drive to Agra; en-route visit Sikandra tomb. On your arrival at Agra, TMI representatives will help you to check in the hotel. Later, visit the Agra Fort complex which features a Pearl Mosque, Diwan-e-am (the hall of public audience) and Diwan-e-Khas (the hall of private audience). Overnight stay at the Agra hotel.


AGRA
In the morning visit the famous Taj Mahal, which was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife, Mumtaz Mahal. The afternoon will be free for leisure. Overnight stay at the Agra hotel.


AGRA – JAIPUR (233 KMS-5 HRS)
Post breakfast at the hotel, drive to Agra. En route stop at Fatehpur Sikri, which features some of the finest examples of Mughal architectural splendor in its complex. Later, continue your drive to Jaipur. On your arrival at Jaipur, TMI representative will help you with the check-in process. Overnight stay at the Jaipur hotel.


JAIPUR
In the morning visit the Amber Fort, which was once the capital of this state long time ago. In the afternoon, go for a sightseeing tour of Jaipur, which includes places like City Palace, Jantar Mantar, and Ram Niwas Garden. Overnight stay at the Jaipur hotel.


JAIPUR – DELHI (260 KMS-6 HRS)
After breakfast at the hotel, check out and board your vehicle to reach Delhi. On arrival, meet our representative who will transfer you to the hotel. Overnight stay at the Delhi hotel.


TRANSFER TO AIRPORT
After breakfast, morning will be free for leisure. In the evening, you will board a flight or train for your journey onwards.
