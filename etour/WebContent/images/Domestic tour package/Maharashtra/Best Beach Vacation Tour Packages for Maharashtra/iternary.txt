Itinerary
MUMBAI / PUNE - ALIBAUG
MUMBAI / PUNE - ALIBAUG (145 KM FROM MUMBAI & 148 KM FROM PUNE)
Early morning leave for Alibaug. On arrival, check-in to the hotel. Take rest for a while and then proceed for sightseeing. Return for dinner. Overnight stay at the Hotel.

ALIBAUG to MURUD
ALIBAUG - MURUD
After breakfast, proceed to Kashid beach and after that to Murud. On arrival, check-in to the Hotel. Evening is at your disposal to explore the nearby places. Overnight stay at the Hotel.

MURUD to MUMBAI / PUNE
MURUD - MUMBAI / PUNE
In the morning, after breakfast proceed for sightseeing. Return and check-out from the Hotel to leave for Mumbai / Pune. On arrival, get down at your place or at Airport / Bus / Railway Station to carry on with your further destination.
